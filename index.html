About BEBBCO

Railway & Defence Vehicle Fabrications

Luxury coaches Fabrications

The Company was established in the year 1983 and is engaged in Body Building of Buses, Tippers, Load Bodies, Defence Vehicles, specialized vehicles and Trailer bodies on wheels.
BEBBCO is very Strong Brand name not only in Jharkhand but also in states like AP, Odisha ,WB, Rajasthan and Delhi for buses body building for State Transport Corporation.
In Body building business BEBBCO has build more than 15000 Units buses and more than 30000 units of Army Load bodies on TATA, Ashok Leyland, Mahindra, BharatBenz, Ural, Eicher chassis.
Bebbco builds anything and everything on wheels.
